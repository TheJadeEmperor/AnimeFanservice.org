“It’s not such a bad thing to receive help from someone else.” Alex walked over to the Thunder Sword, picked it up and placed it in Xiao’s hands.

She had a confused look on her face. “What is the meaning of this?”

“You defeated Ekin so this is yours now. You are now the owner of the Thunder Sword. I’d rather it be you. I don’t want this powerful weapon to fall into the wrong hands.”

She was still in shock, but a minute later, she accepted it. “Thank you, Mr. Cheng, I mean Alex.” [13]

Zi stood up, putting an arm over his injured ribs. “So now we have three of the ultimate weapons, huh? Our team is becoming incredible.”

Just now, Li and Celina arrived with the imperial doctor. The natural thing to say is to ask what happened, but Li already knew within seconds. Ekin is dead, and Xiao has the Thunder Sword, thus what happened was obvious. In her mind, Xiao is a terrifying opponent despite her polite personality.

Celina summoned Dr. Tofu to check out Xiao Ye’s eyes. As the imperial doctor, he is the best in the kingdom, possibly one of the top doctors in the known world. Being a doctor takes years of study of medicine, which includes intensive schooling and a degree after graduation. Needless to say, the imperial doctor is a prestigious title that is the envy of all doctors. Dr. Tofu is the son of a wealthy family that has a lot of power in the northern provinces, and he was recommended by Lord Huong years ago. He was also the same doctor who looked after Celina when she was poisoned, so he is already trusted by her. [14]

Xiao was sitting in bed and Dr. Tofu just finished examining her eyes. Alex walked up to him. “Doctor, how is her condition?”

The doctor rubbed his beard and thought long and hard. “Hmmm, her injury is too old. I’m afraid there is nothing I can do with my current ability.”

“Can’t you do anything? Is she going to be blind her whole life?”

“I am sorry, sir. I cannot create miracles.” Dr. Tofu said this with much regret in his voice. He is over sixty years old and has saved many lives, but modern science has its limitations.

Alex was not about to give up. “Then I will summon the best doctors from the land and have them all examine her.”

Xiao pulled on Alex’s sleeve. “Alex…it is alright. I appreciate everything you and Lady Ce are doing for me. I do not need a doctor. I have already accepted my fate a long time ago.”

“But your sight was unfairly taken away from you.” said Alex.

“No, it is okay. I am used to it. To be honest, I did not expect even the best doctor in the world to be able to help me.”

Alex came outside with a depressed mood. “The doctor cannot do anything. He said that he cannot create miracles.”

Li said “Hmmm, this sure seems hopeless. There must be a way we can help Miss. Ye. Hmmm…” Suddenly, the word miracle gave her an idea, and she pounded her own hand. “I got it! Alex, we can bring her to the temple!”

“The temple?” He gasped. “You mean…”

“The Temple of Enlightenment.”

“Of course!” Alex exclaimed. “With their holy healing powers, they can heal any injury.”

“No matter how old it is.” Li added.

“The Temple of Enlightenment?” Xiao repeated.

“Yes.” replied Li. “The monks there can heal any bodily injury.”

“But isn’t that place just a rumor?” Xiao asked.

“Not at all. My sister and I have been there. But the path there will be dangerous. I remember Zi and I going there and we were almost killed by bandits…alligators… killer bees, and many other things.”

“That’s because you’re both inexperienced. I’ve been there before and I remember the path there. It is worth a try, is it not?”

“Thank you, but I kindly decline.”

“Huh? What?” Li asked dumbfounded.

Xiao said “I am used to living the way I am. You and the others have done much for me already. I have done nothing for you and I can’t accept any more favors.”

“But…” Li was not expecting her to decline her help. After all, Li rarely does anything selflessly. To have her offer rejected so quickly was not something she was prepared for.

“It is safer to remain inside the palace, is it not?”

“If I go with you it will be fine. With your skills and my skills combined we are untouchable.”

“Li, I have already accepted my fate long ago…that I will not be able to see…not with my eyes at least. There are certain things that one has to accept.”

“Don’t tell me your prefer to stay blind?” said Li.

“I do not consider myself to be blind. I am perfectly capable of doing things you normal people can do. But after our fight, you already knew that, didn’t you? I would rather not risk our lives for something that may or may not happen. But thank you for thinking of me, Li.”

Li was at a loss of words. Alex came over. “I’ll talk to her.” His sister nodded and walked away.

Xiao asked “Are you going to convince me to go there too?”

“Miss Ye, I will not force you to do something you do not want. My sister and I thought you might want to have your eyes cured…and return you to the way you were twelve years ago.”

Standing on the rooftop, they were both facing south, where the summer breeze went past them.

“The truth is…” Xiao pulled out her sword slightly. “Whether I see or not makes no difference. Perhaps it is better to stay this way. I have seen many ugly and grotesque things in this world. When I lost my vision I saw nothing but dark, red blood for a few days. My hometown is the Yi Ling southern district. Many crimes happen due to poverty over there.”

“That certainly is true. You may not want to see again.” said Alex. “But do you think your life was so bad before? At least you had a brother who cared for you. He even resorted to crime just to get money to get you a doctor. What Jiang did was certainly not right, but the point is that he did it for you.”

Xiao listened to him attentively. He’s speaking in the same wise way that he spoke to Celina when she was about to commit suicide. Alex does not even know he has this natural skill of making people see things from a different perspective. It is a rare leadership quality indeed.

Alex continued: “It is true that the world is an ugly place. I have been some horrible childhood experiences myself. I used to have a scar on my right arm that prohibited me from using a sword properly. But thanks to the monks at the temple, that old scar is gone. If it wasn’t for them, I would not be who I am today. The world is filled with ugliness, death and despair, but on the flip side, it is full of beauty, life and grace. Perhaps you were too focused on the bad things to remember the good things. Sight is one of God’s greatest gifts to us humans. It is better to see than to not see. At least in my opinion. But ultimately, the choice is yours.” He walked away.

Xiao was seriously considering his words. They certainly have some truth to them. She’s always been able to read other people, but now she is the one being read. And she had to be honest to herself. Is she really happy with the way she is?

Xiao went to visit Jiang in his room. “I am going now, brother.” she said.

“Xiao, I’ve always pictured what you would look like when your eyes are open. I remember them clearly…they were the most beautiful eyes I’ve ever seen.”

Not knowing how to respond to his comment, she changed the subject. “How are your wounds?”

“I’m fine.” Jiang replied, but his voice indicated otherwise.

“You’re lying again, brother.” She touched his shoulder and he groaned in pain.

“Ughhh…”

“You should come with us. The monks can heal your wounds.”

“No, you go ahead.” said Jiang. “In my condition, I will only slow you down. This journey is for you. Besides, your brother is the first student of the War God. All I need is some rest and I will be good as new.”

Xiao smiled and became at ease because this time he is telling the truth.

Li and Xiao stood at the front gate of the imperial complex. Two guards pull the gigantic doors open.

Celina: “Good luck, you two. Take care.”

Li: “We will. I will take good care of Xiao.”

Xiao bowed. “Thank you all for your care. I am indebted to you.”

Alex: “Sister, wait.” He handed them the imperial seal. “Just in case you run into trouble.”

Li took it and smiled. “Thank you, Alex.”

Just as they walk out, Jiang came with the aid of his friend Sui Long.

“Xiao…”

“Brother…”

“Be careful out there.” said Jiang.

“Thank you, brother.” Xiao hugged him. “I will come back and see your face with my own eyes.”

“I’ll be waiting for that day.” Jiang replied and grunted in pain.

Sui: “C’mon now, let’s get you back to bed.”

The two women leave the front gate and the guards closed the door. Creeek. The last thing they saw was Xiao and Li waving happily.

Nighttime came shortly afterwards. Li suggests they go to a stable to buy horses. The owner is an old man with a short beard. He came to greet his customers. “Ho ho, what can I do for you two young ladies?”

Li: “We’d like two horses, the fastest ones you got.”

“Sure thing.” replied the owner. He led them to a certain part of the stable. A horse is locked up in a wooden stall. “These will do.”

“Thank you. How much will it cost?”

“I see that you two are in a hurry. Just two silver coins will do.”

“Wow, so cheap?” said Li. “Thank you so much.”

The old man replied by laughing.

As Li and Xiao were on their way out, they say thank you once more and went on their way. They pulled horse’s reigns to make the horses move with them. They will ride the horses once they leave the city.

The old man went back to the stable. A man was waiting for him in the darkness. He jumped off the ceiling and revealed himself.

“Ah. You startled me.” said the owner.
